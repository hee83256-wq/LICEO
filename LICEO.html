About Us

Institutional History

Founded in 1955 by Atty. Rodolfo N. Pelaez and family; non‑sectarian, private higher education institution in Cagayan de Oro City.

Vision, Mission & Core Values

Vision: Leading university producing globally competitive, morally upright professionals.
Mission: Quality instruction, research, extension; preserve Filipino culture.
Values: Competence • Conscience • Commitment

Leadership & Administration

University President, Board of Trustees, Academic Council, Deans, Department Heads — organizational chart available upon request.

Accreditations & Partnerships

PAASCU Accredited • ISO 9001:2015 • CHED Recognized • Local & International Academic Partners

Campus Facilities

Main Campus — Rodolfo N. Pelaez Blvd; Library, Laboratories, Auditorium, Sports Complex, Student Center, Health Services.
